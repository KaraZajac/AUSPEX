## Source-monitoring posture for AUSPEX

- **Highest signal cadence**: Treasury OFAC press releases (irregular, but each one carries gold-standard doctrine-attestation language); FBI IC3 PSAs (each major crypto-theft event); Chainalysis annual report (Dec/Jan); Microsoft MSTIC blogs (irregular but high-value naming).
- **Watch for replacement of UN PoE**: MSMT publications are the new must-ingest. State Dept Jan 2026 release is the first consolidated post-PoE assessment.
- **DOJ indictments**: rare but each provides retroactive consolidation of attribution and explicit naming of RGB unit alignment — treat as canonical authority for actor mapping.
- **Vendor follow-on within ~48h of any major event**: Mandiant, Microsoft, CrowdStrike, Elliptic, Chainalysis typically issue technical write-ups within 1–3 days of any major DPRK incident; this is when actor-cluster naming gets fixed.
