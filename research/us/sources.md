## How to use these sources for ongoing AUSPEX tracking

- **Daily**: The Record, CyberScoop, DefenseScoop, Cybercom.mil press releases
- **Weekly**: CISA AA advisories, DOJ press releases, OFAC actions
- **Quarterly**: ODNI testimony, CRS updates, Lawfare deep-dives
- **Annual**: ODNI ATA, CSC 2.0 implementation report, ONCD/NCS implementation reporting
- **As triggered**: NSArchive new FOIA releases (irregular but high-value), major leak/breach disclosures

## Notable gaps (no good primary source)

- NSPM-13 full text (classified)
- Current TAO/CCI organizational structure (post-2013 reorgs only partially public)
- Real-time tally of Hunt Forward deployments (CYBERCOM releases cumulative numbers in posture statements, not country-by-country running list)
- Specific zero-days in current CYBERCOM/NSA arsenal (VEP process opaque)
